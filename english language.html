Tech Zone Certificate In English Language Course




Course Content


English Language is very important because it is one of the most widely spoken languages in the world. Learning English offers people some benefits such as getting better jobs, communication with others everywhere, and helps people be more confident. Being confidence means that a person has the opportunity to express his or her ideas and opinions. If a person goes to the hospital, airport, office, or any other important place, he or she will feel more comfortable. For all these reasons, English has the power to make people/students more confident. “Step in as a learner, step out as a fluent speaker”


This English Language Course is for students who have some basic English but are not at the level of the main Interchange Course. This will take students with basic English to the level of Interchange 1.


The exact content of the courses is designed around the specific needs and wishes of the individual participant, but most English training courses include elements of vocabulary-building, grammar, conversational skills, and reading and writing. The communication-based training focuses on speaking and listening skills to ensure effective verbal communication. This training method requires you to actively speak from the first lesson onwards.


Training in intercultural issues naturally plays an integral part in my courses as my clients invariably deal with international business partners, customers and colleagues.
